Sesiones 7-8

Oral Presentation

 ORAL PRESENTATION
Present your project
Work with your group and present your city to the class.

 
🟦 What to do
Speak in English
Use your presentation (Google Slides or Canva)
Explain your city clearly
 
🟨 What to include
Name of the city and country
Monuments and famous places
Food and interesting facts
Comparatives and superlatives
 
🟪 Tips
Speak slowly and clearly
Look at your classmates
Do not read all the time
Practice before presenting
 
🟧 Group work
All students must participate
Each student must speak
 

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Criterio de evaluación:

CE.LE.3.4 Expresarse oralmente de forma comprensible utilizando recursos lingüísticos básicos.
