Amazing Cities Project

5º Primaria – Inglés

Sesión 4

Búsqueda de información sobre una ciudad

Choose a city and create your project:

Work in groups.
Choose one city from the list.
Find information about your city.
Look for monuments, food, culture and famous places.
Write sentences using comparatives and superlatives.
Prepare a digital presentation and present it to the class.


Use these links to look for the information about your city:

https://kids.nationalgeographic.com
https://www.ducksters.com
https://www.kiddle.co
https://www.britannica.com/kids
https://www.visitacity.com
https://www.google.com

Criterio de evaluación:

CE.LE.3.3 Producir textos escritos breves y sencillos adecuados a la situación comunicativa.
