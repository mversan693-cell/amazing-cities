Sesiones 5-6

Creación de una presentación digital

DIGITAL PRESENTATION TASK


Create your presentation:
Work with your group and create a digital presentation using Google Slides or Canva.
All the work will be done in class.


Before starting
First, you will write a draft in class.
The teacher will check your work.
After correction, you will create the final digital presentation.

Structure of the presentation:


Slide 1: Introduction

Name of the city
Country
Basic information about the city
 
Slides 2–6: Comparatives

Compare places, food or monuments
Use comparatives (bigger than, more interesting than…)
Example:
Paris is more beautiful than London.

 
Slides 7–11: Superlatives

Describe the most important things
Use superlatives (the biggest, the most famous…)
Example:
The Eiffel Tower is the most famous monument.

 
🟪 What to include:
Monuments
Food
Famous places
Images
 
🟧 Important
Use simple English
Work in your group
Follow the structure
Do the work in class (not at home)
 
🟫 Final task
Present your project to the class.


 

 

 ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Criterio de evaluación:

CE.LE.3.5 Crear contenidos digitales sencillos integrando información en distintos formatos y utilizando herramientas digitales de forma segura.
